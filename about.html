About Me


Short Bio

I was born and raised in Knoxville, TN for most of my life... . . .


We moved to Florida briefly when I was a child. I'e stayed in Atlanta, GA for a while. Hickory, North Carolina was briefly my home. I've stayed in Manhattan, NY briefly too. I was born in 1977 and the world was a much better place then. I was just a child when I took up a liking for the arts and music. I went to grade school and graduated. I went on to college. I have 350 plus credit hours of undergrad work and no diploma yet. I have certificates but no diploma. I am still in college studying Graphic Design/Web Design. The worst of worst happened to me and the best of the best in life!


I am an artist and a designer. I'm just budding in this world. It took me along time to get here. Things get better all the time. I get wiser with time and experience.If I knew then what I know now, I would've budded sooner I think.

I am slowly putting my portfolio on the internet and making websites for people as well. I wanted to be a Social Influencer, but I have changed gears.

I am a mother as well.
